IoT Project:
Gas Leakage & Fire Detector

| Jan 2024 |

Collaborated with a 2-person team on an IoT project to develop a gas leakage and fire detection system. The project utilized sensors and Arduino IDE software, with data transmitted via an MQTT server. We integrated MongoDB for data storage and utilized Google Cloud Platform for enhanced processing and analytics. My contributions included designing and implementing key system components, ensuring reliable detection and alerting mechanisms.
