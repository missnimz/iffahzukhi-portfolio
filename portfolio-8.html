USM Bus Tracker (FYP)

| Oct 2023 - June 2024 |

I developed the frontend of a mobile application using Flutter, integrating sensors connected via LoRaWAN to provide real-time data from the backend (using NodeRED server, InfluxDB, and PostgreSQL). The app calculated distances to bus stops and estimated bus arrival times (ETA). I also implemented a notification system using Firebase Cloud Messaging to inform bus operators about students waiting at each stop. This innovative project earned a Silver Award for its impact.
