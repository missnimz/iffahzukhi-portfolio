USM Bus Tracker (FYP)

| Oct 2023 - June 2024 |

Developed the frontend of a mobile application using Flutter that utilized sensors to deliver real-time data from the backend, calculating distances to bus stops and estimating arrival times (ETA) with LoRaWAN technology. Created a notification system for bus operators to receive updates of students waiting at each stop. This project was recognized for its innovation and received a Silver Award.
